## Business Insights

### 1. Loss Ratio and Risk Analysis
- The **Health** product represents the highest loss ratio at **248.79%**, indicating that it returns the largest share of premiums back as claim payments. This product requires premium rate reviews.
- The **Auto** product has the lowest loss ratio at **180.84%**, making it the most profitable product line in the portfolio.

### 2. Profitability and Growth
- **Net Profits** are led by the **Home** segment with a net of **₹-5,639,083.00**.
- Dynamic adjustments to underwriting limits, particularly in segments with high outliers, will shield net reserves.

### 3. Claims Fraud Susceptibility
- **Home** policies suffer from the highest rate of fraud at **19.12%** of all claims.
- Claims audit triggers should prioritize **Home** claims, particularly those filed within short intervals after policy activation.
